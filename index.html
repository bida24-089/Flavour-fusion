Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Add egg, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Stir until smooth.
  4. Cook on a heated pan until golden brown.
